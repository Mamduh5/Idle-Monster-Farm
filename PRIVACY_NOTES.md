# Privacy Notes

These are developer notes, not a legal privacy policy.

- Idle Monster Farm stores game progress locally on the device/browser.
- Android builds may use AdMob rewarded ads when configured.
- Browser/dev builds can use mock rewarded ads.
- The game does not currently include a custom backend, cloud save, account login, analytics, or in-app purchases.
- A production Play Store release should include a complete privacy policy and Data Safety answers for AdMob.
